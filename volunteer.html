Volunteer with Paw Haven Rescue

At Paw Haven Rescue, we believe every paw deserves a chance. Our mission is to provide love, safety, and second chances to animals in need — but we can’t do it alone. Volunteers are the heart of our rescue family, and your time and compassion can truly change lives.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a difference: Help animals find the loving homes they deserve.
  • Learn & grow: Gain hands-on experience in animal care, rescue operations, and community outreach.
  • Build connections: Join a community of passionate animal lovers who share your dedication.
  • Share love: Whether it’s walking a dog, comforting a shy cat, or helping at events, your kindness means the world.

Volunteer Opportunities

We welcome people of all skills and backgrounds! Some ways you can help:

  • Animal Care: Feeding, grooming, walking, and giving the animals the affection they need.
  • Foster Care: Provide a temporary, loving home until animals are adopted.
  • Photography & Media: Capture moments to help promote adoptions and awareness.
  • Events & Fundraising: Assist with adoption days, awareness events, and fundraising activities.
  • Logistics & Support: Organize supplies, help with transport, or lend a hand at our rescue center.

How to Join Us

  1. Fill out our Volunteer Form.
  2. Attend a short orientation to learn about our rescue and safety practices.
  3. Start making a difference!
