Welcome to Paw Haven Rescue

At Paw Haven Rescue, every pawprint tells a story of hope, healing, and second chances. We’re more than a shelter — we’re a sanctuary where abandoned and mistreated animals find safety, care, and the love they deserve.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need, while building a compassionate community of adopters, volunteers, and donors who believe every life matters.

Join the Mission. Change a Life. Every click brings us closer to a forever home. Explore our adoptable pets, sign up to volunteer, or make a donation that helps us rescue and rehabilitate animals in need. Visit our website today and become a hero to a furry friend.
