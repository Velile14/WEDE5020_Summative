Adoption process

We’re thrilled that you’re considering giving a rescued animal a loving home. Our adoption process is designed to ensure the best match between pets and adopters, while maintaining transparency, care, and efficiency.

    1.Apply Online

    Start by filling out our adoption application. It helps us understand your lifestyle, preferences, and what kind of companion you're looking for.

    2.Screening & Interview

    We’ll review your application and follow up with a short interview to clarify details and answer your questions. It’s a chance to explore compatibility and expectations.

    3.Home Check

    To ensure a safe environment, we’ll conduct a home visit or virtual tour. This helps us support long-term success and prepare your space for your new pet.

    4.Meet & Greet

    You’ll meet your potential pet in a calm setting to see how you connect. We encourage bringing family members or other pets to ensure harmony.

    5.Final Approval & Fee

    Once approved, you’ll pay the adoption fee, which covers medical care and essentials. We’ll walk you through the final paperwork.

    6.Adoption Agreement

    You’ll sign a shared commitment to your pet’s well-being. It outlines care guidelines and our ongoing support.

    7.Welcome Home

    We’ll coordinate pickup or delivery and provide a starter kit with food, tips, and contact info. We’re here to help as you settle in.

    8.Follow-Up Support

    We’ll check in at key milestones to ensure everything’s going smoothly. Our team remains available for advice and encouragement.
