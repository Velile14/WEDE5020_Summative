About Us - Paw Haven Rescue

At Paw Haven Rescue, we believe every animal deserves a second chance — a safe haven, a loving home, and a life filled with dignity. Founded by a passionate team of animal advocates, our mission is simple yet powerful: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned and vulnerable animals across South Africa.

We operate with compassion, transparency, and a deep commitment to ethical care. From emergency rescues to long-term rehabilitation, our dedicated volunteers and veterinary partners work tirelessly to ensure every paw we save walks toward a brighter future.

What we do:

We respond to urgent cases, providing shelter and veterinary treatment to animals in need.

We match rescued pets with loving families and offer temporary foster care for those in transition.

We promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare awareness through education and advocacy.

Our growing community of volunteers, donors, and partners makes our mission possible.

Whether you're here to adopt, volunteer, donate, or simply learn more — welcome to the Paw Haven family. Together, we’re rewriting the stories of countless animals, one rescue at a time.
